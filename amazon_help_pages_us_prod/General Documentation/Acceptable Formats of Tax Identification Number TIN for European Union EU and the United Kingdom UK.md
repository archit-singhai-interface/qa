Each EU country and the UK has a specific format of TIN for Individual and
Business sellers. For further support on this information, contact your tax
advisor. You may also refer to the below documentation published by authorized
bodies:

  * [Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD)](https://www.oecd.org/tax/automatic-exchange/crs-implementation-and-assistance/tax-identification-numbers/)
  * Europe Commission (EC) online check module to check the TIN syntax: [TIN online check module](https://ec.europa.eu/taxation_customs/tin) and [TINs by country](https://ec.europa.eu/taxation_customs/tin/#/tin-country)

If you're a seller selling products on Amazon's EU and UK stores, you are
required to provide your TIN by following the steps below on Seller Central.  

  1. Go to **Settings** and select **Account Info**.
  2. Select **Tax information from the left menu** , then click **VAT/GST Registration Number**.
  3. Under **List all VAT/GST registration numbers** tab, you can add the **Tax Identification Number**

**Acceptable formats for VAT (Value Added Tax)**

**Note:** Each EU member country has a slightly different format for their VAT
number system, featuring a variation of numbers and letters. It is important
that sellers take close care in using the correct format as the numbers are
checked against VIES (VAT information exchange system).

For further information, contact your tax advisor.
